How do I get started as a freelance writer?

The best thing you can do is to get started before you ever leave your current job. When freelancing comes with so much uncertainty, its nice to get the ball rolling when you still have the comfort and security of a steady paycheck. Use that time to build up a side hustle and also take care of those nuts and bolts things you need to get startedlike setting up a website and an accounting system, for example.
